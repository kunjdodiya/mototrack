MotoTrack is a no-frills GPS ride tracker built for motorcyclists.

Hit Record before you set off. Lock your phone, ride, and stop when you're done. MotoTrack captures the whole route — distance, duration, top speed, average speed, elevation gain, peak lean angle, and the line you actually rode — and saves it the second you tap Stop.

Why it's different:

• Local-first. Every ride is written to your phone first. A dropped signal, a closed tunnel, a foreign SIM — none of it loses your ride.
• Background recording that actually works. On Android we run a foreground service so the GPS keeps logging when the screen locks. On iOS we use the proper background-location entitlement.
• One Google sign-in, your rides everywhere. Sign in with Google once and your rides sync to your private cloud automatically. New phone? Sign in, your history is there.
• Lean angle estimated from your GPS trajectory. No external sensors needed.
• Speed graph + map + stats baked into a 1080×1620 share image, ready for Instagram or your group chat.
• Multi-bike support. Register the bikes in your stable, tag each ride.
• Free of ads, trackers, and creepy "social" features. We never sell your data; we never share it.

Permissions we ask for and why:

• Location (Always / Background) — to record your ride continuously, including when the phone is in your tank bag with the screen off.
• Notifications (Android) — to show the persistent "recording your ride" notification while a ride is in progress.

What we DON'T ask for: contacts, microphone, photos, calendar, or any advertising id.

Read the full privacy policy at https://mototrack.pages.dev/privacy
